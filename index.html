Julia Lazzari-Dean, Ph.D.

I'm a microscopist. I'm passionate about building and using new microscopy technologies, and my work lies somewhere at the intersection of biophysics, chemical biology & cell biology.

I develop new technologies to "watch" the foundational (and beautiful!) processes going on inside cells. I love quantitative microscopy and live cell imaging, and I particularly love when the two of those are combined.

You can see a current list of my publications on ORCID and my code on GitHub.

Social media isn't my strong suit, but I try to keep Twitter and LinkedIn reasonably up-to-date.

If we share interests, I'd love to hear from you! -Julia
